Bill Gates

“Success is a lousy teacher. It seduces smart people into thinking they can’t lose.” -Bill Gates

  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, Washington.
  • 1973 - Enrolls at Harvard College after scoring 1590/1600 in the SAT.
  • 1975 - Leaves Harvard to go work at MITS (Micro Instrumentation and Telemetry Systems) with Paul Allen.
  • 1976 - The trade name "Microsoft" is registered with the Office of the Secretary of the State of New Mexico as result of a partnership of Gates with Allen.
  • 1980 - Microsoft is approached by IBM. Microsoft delivers PC DOS as an adaptation of the already existing 86-DOS operating system. Despite IBM's name on the new operating system the press quickly identify Microsoft as very influential.
  • 1985 - Microsoft launches its first retail version of Microsoft Windows.
  • 1989 - Last shipped piece of code actually written by Mr. Gates.
  • 1994 - Marries Melinda French. Creates the "William H. Gates Foundation".
  • 2000 - Along with his wife, Mr. Gates combined three family foundations to create the charitable "Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation".
  • 2006 - Announces he will transition out of his day-to-day role over the next two years to dedicate more time to philanthropy.
  • Present - Billionaire philantropist by day, masked superhero by night.
